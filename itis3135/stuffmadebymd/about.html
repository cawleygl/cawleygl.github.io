About Us

Founded in 2022, Stuff Made by MD produces handmade gifts and lifestyle products for consumers online through Etsy and through their storefront at Painted Tree in Charlotte, NC. The store's founder, Mary Dare Martin, designs and creates each handmade piece with her own personal style.
